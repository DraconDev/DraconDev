## 🛡️ Source Policy Logic

1. 🌕 **OPEN SOURCE (MIT):** Used for "Magnets" (Junk-Runner) and small utility tools (Autopub). These build your brand and prove your technical depth to the widest audience.
2. 🌓 **SOURCE AVAILABLE (Dracon License):** Used for "Strong Engines" (Azumi, Terma, Axiom) and the "Operating System" (Code, Utilities). This builds trust through visibility while protecting your commercial rights.
3. 🌑 **CLOSED SOURCE (Proprietary):** Used for "Special Sauce" factories (AI Book Writer, Media Registry). These are your competitive secrets that power your services.
4. 🌊 **LEAKY CLIENTS (Visible):** Used for Browser Extensions (SamAI). The code is visible by nature, but we provide no license for redistribution, keeping the value in the Backend/Profiles.
