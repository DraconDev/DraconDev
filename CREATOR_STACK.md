# 🎬 CREATOR STACK: Media & Registry

Professional tools for the modern creator, built with the same Sovereign principles as our engineering core.

## 🎥 AI Video Editor
**Automated Production Pipeline.**
A CLI and GUI tool designed for "Zero-Manual" video editing. Drop in raw footage; get a polished release.
- **Silence Trimming:** Automatically identifies and removes dead air using dB threshold analysis.
- **Filler Word Removal:** Uses AI to scrub "um", "uh", and "ah" from your recordings.
- **Auto-Reframe:** Intelligent 9:16 vertical cropping for TikTok/Shorts.
- **YouTube Presets:** Automates intro/outro addition, background music mixing, and chapter generation.

## 📦 Warehouse
**Content-Addressed Media Registry.**
Think of it as **"Git LFS for Content Creators."** A robust system for storing, versioning, and distributing large media artifacts.
- **BLAKE3 Deduplication:** Store the same file once, regardless of where it appears in your catalog.
- **Cryptographic Integrity:** Every release is verified by hash. No more "silent corruption" of old backups.
- **Searchable Catalog:** Full-text search over your entire history of books, videos, and courses.
- **Ingest-Only:** Focused on storage and distribution stability, not ephemeral rendering.
